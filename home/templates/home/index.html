What Combat Canvas aims to do

Our website is designed to bring veterans together through art.
Art is a great form of self expression and often used as a theraputic tool.
Here at Combat Canvas you can not only veiw art, but be encouraged to create projects yourself!
Using our log in feature you can create a profile and upload your own creations and comment on eachothers to encourage them.
Why not give it a go?


Why Chose Art Therapy?

Uk armed forces personnel who are currently surving are not offered art therapy as a potential theraputic tool. Our platform aims to bridge that gap and encourage the use of art to aid veterans mental health and combat mental health challenges such as PTSD, anxiety, depression and other stress related conditions. In our footer you can find other helpful resources adressing the support avalible to veterans.

Where do I begin?

After you've created your user profile you will have access to our online community of vetarans where you can veiw eachothers art and encourage one another. Don't worry if you've never done art before as we will send you an artists starter kit with examples to get you started after you've signed up! We encourage you you to find a safe, quiet and confortable space to do your project and keep talking to the community for help and guidence.
